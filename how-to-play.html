Five Hundred Credits

How to Play:

Game Type: Simulation Game

Age: 12+

No. of Player: 1

  1. Pick a faction. The player would need to pick a faction that he/she would like to represent.
  2. Read the narratives. There would be a narrative after every option which sets the background and context for the player. Remember to read them!
  3. Clear the stages. The game consists of a few stages. To advance, the player has to either use the image and/or given text toanswer an ethical question that would determine their points. Choose wisely as it can affect the overall points you have.
  4. All the best! Lastly do not forget to have fun!
