Unfortunate Accident

You are involved in an accident (along with another car), resulting in the death of a pedestrian. You are aware that you are the one at fault but the other party thinks they are in the wrong. Do you let them think it is their fault and cause them to lose 100 credits? (0 credit deduction) However, there is a possibility that you get caught by surveillance. If you do get caught by surveillance, you will suffer a deduction of 150 credit. Or do you admit that it is your fault and lose -100 credits?
