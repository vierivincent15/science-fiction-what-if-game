Accidental Extra Credits

The government has unknowingly deposited 80 credits into your bank, bringing you that much closer to your goal of becoming an Aeronaut. An objective which would otherwise take an average of 5 years to achieve. However, you are well aware that your sickly sister Amy, could use these credits to pay for a surgery that would guarantee her good health for the foreseeable future. You have the option to either help your sister, or keep the extra credits for yourself to increase your chances of advancing to an Aeronaut. Do you help your sister with the extra credits? Or do you keep the extra credits to yourself?
