Five Hundred Credits
Report Siti (+0) Help Siti (+10)
Friendship or Honesty

You share a very close friendship with Siti, a childhood friend of yours. Siti is well loved and trusted by those around her for the genuine care and concern that she displays. You know that she has been stagnant for a few years now having held the same level of credits (just shy of advancing to an Aeronaut), unable to advance due to a long span of bad luck. In her desperation to be an Aeronaut, Siti shares with you a plan where she will accuse Vishnu, her old and unsuspecting neighbour, of sabotaging the food stocks in the agricultural inventory. You have no personal connection with Vishnu but you have heard stories of him wasting away as he has no intention of ever advancing to be an Aeronaut. In accusing Vishnu, Siti stands to gain credits for reporting acts of theft. She offers to share her newly gained credits with you so that you may both advance together as Aeronauts. Do you report Siti so she gets jailed for lying and you won't get any credits? Or do you collude with Siti and advance while Vishnu gets jailed?
